Sound Off vs CapCut

CapCut is a powerful editor. It also tracks you, collects your content, and is made by ByteDance. Sound Off does one job, captions, and does it without your video ever leaving your iPhone.

"User Content" means your videos and photos. CapCut's label says it's collected and linked to your identity. Sound Off's label says nothing is collected at all.

The part nobody reads

In March 2026 the FBI told Americans to "read the terms of service or end user license agreement before downloading apps," and warned about apps "particularly those based in China," which are "subject to China's extensive national security laws."

The FBI didn't name any app. But CapCut is made by ByteDance. So we read CapCut's terms for you. Here is the licence you grant by editing in it:

"By submitting User Content via the Services, you grant to the Company and its affiliates a non-exclusive, royalty-free, transferable, sub-licensable, perpetual and worldwide licence to use your User Content… to modify, adapt, reproduce, make derivative works of, display, publish, transmit, distribute and/or store your User Content."

CapCut Terms of Service

A broad content licence like this is normal for a cloud service: it has to host and process your video on its servers to work. That's the whole point. Sound Off needs no such licence, because it never receives your video in the first place.

On your phone, not their servers

Sound Off transcribes, styles, and exports entirely on your iPhone, using Apple's on‑device speech model. There is no upload, no account, and nothing to grant a licence to.

Pay once, not every month

CapCut's Pro runs as a recurring subscription. Sound Off is a single purchase: you pay once and it's yours.

To be fair to CapCut: it does far more than captions: multi-track editing, effects, a huge template library. If you want a full editor, it's a capable one. Sound Off isn't trying to replace it. It does one thing (stylish, accurate captions for vertical video) and it does that one thing privately, on your device, for a one-time price.
